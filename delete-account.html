Delete Your Nexum Account

Manage your account deletion directly from the app

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Nexum account and all associated data directly from within the app:

  1. Open the Nexum app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (found in the Profile tab)
  3. Select "Privacy & Data"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Review the information and confirm your decision

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

⚠️ Important Information

  • All personal data will be permanently deleted
  • Your game progress and achievements will be removed
  • Access to any purchased content will be revoked
  • This action cannot be undone

Data Deletion Process

After initiating account deletion:

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • Your personal data will be permanently deleted in accordance with our privacy policy
  • This action cannot be undone once completed
