How to Export Your Data

You can export a copy of your personal data at any time through the Nexum app:

  1. Open the Nexum app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (found in the Profile tab)
  3. Select "Privacy & Data"
  4. Tap "Export My Data"
  5. Your data will be prepared and downloaded to your device

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and similar privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability - Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object - Object to certain processing of your data
